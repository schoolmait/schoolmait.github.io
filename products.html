Student Wellbeing Survey

A Comprehensive Student Wellbeing Assessment Solution

This product allows schools to evaluate students' wellbeing through a set of online surveys. A multi-perspective approach involves students, teachers and parents, to provide a holistic view of the wellbeing within school. The comprehensive analysis measures social, emotional and physical wellbeing of every student and signposts possible evidence-based interventions inspired by recent research in order to drive positive change.

Product Goals

  • To provide a holistic view of student wellbeing levels in school, each class and every student, and identify areas of strength and areas for improvement.
  • To signpost possible evidence-based interventions and promote better wellbeing among students.

Ideal for

Primary schools (Ages 8+), Secondary schools, Universities

Required Resources

  • School Admin / IT lead / wellbeing lead - 2-3 hours for survey setup and communications to participants
  • Teachers – up to 30 min to answer the survey per class
  • Students – up to 10 min to complete the survey
  • Parents – up to 5 min contribution per student
  • Participants' access to connected devices is required (smartphones / tablets / laptops / computers)
  • Additional staff time needs to be allocated to review the reports, prepare and execute intervention plans

Privacy

We collect student aliases, instead of real names, so that school staff can recognize and support every student, while keeping student identities safer.

Teachers' Wellbeing Survey

A Comprehensive Teachers' Wellbeing Assessment Solution

This product enable schools to measure teachers' wellbeing through an online survey. By gaining insight into teachers' social, emotional and physical wellbeing, schools can better align their wellbeing strategies for teachers needs and improve retention rates and performance. Moreover, improving teachers' wellbeing can lead to higher levels of students' wellbeing and vice versa, as those are interconnected and mutually influential.

Product Goal

  • To provide a comprehensive view of teachers' wellbeing levels and identify areas of strength and areas for improvement.

Ideal for

Primary schools, Secondary schools

Required Resources

  • Survey lead - 1 hour for communication to participants
  • Teachers – up to 10 min to answer the survey per teacher
  • Additional time will be needed to review the report, prepare and execute intervention plans

Privacy

The teachers' survey is conducted anonymously.
