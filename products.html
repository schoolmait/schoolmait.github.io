Student Wellbeing Survey

A Comprehensive Student Wellbeing Assessment Solution

Product Goals

  • Evaluate students' wellbeing through a set of online surveys to students, teachers and parents.
  • Provide a holistic view of social, emotional, physical and digital wellbeing levels in school.
  • Identify areas of strength and areas for improvement, and signpost possible evidence-based interventions.

Ideal for

Primary schools and Secondary schools (starting age 8+)

Required Resources

  • Before the survey - 2-3 hours from School Admin / Wellbeing Lead to setup the survey and communicate to participants
  • During the survey - the survey takes approximately 10 minutes for students, 30 min for teachers and 5 minutes for parents to complete
  • After the survey - additional staff time is needed to review the reports, prepare and execute intervention plans
  • Participants' access to connected devices is required (smartphones / tablets / laptops / computers)

Privacy

We collect student aliases, instead of real names, so that school staff can recognize and support every student, while keeping student identities safer.

Teachers' Wellbeing Survey

A Comprehensive Teachers' Wellbeing Assessment Solution

Product Goals

  • Evaluate teachers' wellbeing through an online survey for teachers and school leaders.
  • Provide a holistic view of social, emotional, and physical wellbeing levels among staff.
  • Identify areas of strength and areas for improvement, and signpost possible evidence-based interventions.

Ideal for

Primary schools and Secondary schools

Required Resources

  • Before the survey - 1 hour from Survey Lead to set up the survey and communicate to participants
  • During the survey - the survey takes up to 10 minutes per teacher to complete
  • After the survey - additional staff time is needed to review the reports, prepare and execute intervention plans
  • Participants' access to connected devices is required (smartphones / tablets / laptops / computers)

Privacy

The teachers' survey is conducted anonymously to ensure privacy and encourage honest responses.
