Our 8 Wellbeing Elements

These are the core elements we measure:

Safety & Belonging Having a secure, supportive school environment.
Meaning & Purpose Finding purpose in the world around and staying motivated.
Physical Wellbeing Maintaining a healthy diet, exercise, and sleep habits.
Digital Wellbeing Maintaining healthy screen time management and online behavior.
Emotion Management Having skills to understand, regulate, and express emotions effectively.
Peer Relations Building healthy and supportive relationships with peers.
Student-Teacher Relations Having positive and supportive relationships with teachers.
Resilience Having the ability to adapt to stress and challenging experiences.
